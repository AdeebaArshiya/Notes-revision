Your Fat-Burning Routine: What to Do Before and After Exercise

Before your workout, it’s important to prepare your body. A light warm-up and stretching get your muscles ready, boost blood flow, and prevent injuries. Eating something small and balanced, like fruit or a handful of nuts, can give you the right energy to perform better.

After your workout, your body needs recovery. Cooling down with stretches helps relax your muscles and reduce soreness. Drinking water keeps you hydrated, and a protein-rich snack or meal supports muscle repair and keeps your metabolism active, which is key for fat loss.

Consistency in both pre- and post-workout habits will not only help you burn fat but also keep you healthier, stronger, and more energized every day
